01 / A new architecture

01 — Investment Thesis

The arrangements that layer value in the world today have outlived their use.

Investment banks, venture capitalists, brokers, exchanges and capital markets were built for another era. Each layer adds friction, opacity and distance between people and the value they create.

And the system has become captured.

Abaku invests in its replacement: modern financial systems rebuilt on blockchain—open, programmable and designed to move value as naturally as information. It's just infrastructure, and

money should serve everyone equally

04 — Etymology

The word a'ba-ku originates with the Akkadian language and means “to pay”. More generally it conveys the notion of settling a transaction or trade.

We believe it is important to recognise that the business of trading value between men dates back to the earliest memory in human history. It thus seemed appropriate to christen the birth of our most economic models with the most ancient of words.
